The Champlain College Shuttle Tracker for Android was developed by David Watkins (Champlain '13) as an unofficial mobile alternative to the online shuttle tracker available at shuttle.champlain.edu. This app is neither sponsored nor recognized by the college.

The functionality of this app is completely dependent upon that of the online tracker. GPS location services are provided by The Radio North Group, Inc.

If you would like to contact the developer or report a bug, please email david@davidofwatkins.com. Thank you.
